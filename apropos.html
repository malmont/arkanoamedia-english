About

Arkanoa Media is an LLC registered in Florida, specializing in digital services and the IIZIPRO suite. We operate from our headquarters located at 7901 4th St N #19787, Saint Petersburg, Florida, 33702 FL.

The Birth of IIZIPRO

It all started with a ready-to-wear store. We were facing a frustrating limitation: traditional management tools did not suit our needs and the available sales channels were too restrictive.

This experience pushed us to look for a solution, but nothing on the market truly met our expectations. That’s when the idea to create something new was born. With a clear vision—to create tools that are simple, accessible, and intuitive—we embarked on our development journey.

The goal was ambitious: to design a solution that could not only simplify inventory management but also integrate future revenue projections based on key data such as purchases, selling prices, and sales multipliers.

Yet the real challenge was to bring all these tools together into one seamless and coherent suite. IIZIPRO is the result of that challenge—a complete suite designed for retailers and entrepreneurs like us who need powerful yet simple tools to manage their business and plan for the future.

Our Mission

At Arkanoa Media, our mission is to revolutionize the way companies manage and grow their business through innovative digital tools and tailored services. With our suite of solutions, including IIZIPRO, we simplify business management and provide financial performance projections by offering a set of powerful, intuitive tools designed for modern retailers and entrepreneurs.

At the same time, we offer a full range of digital services—from web and app development to digital marketing strategy and branding. Our ambition is to deliver customized solutions that empower our clients to maximize their potential, conquer new markets, and adapt to emerging technological trends.

We firmly believe that innovation and simplicity can transform challenges into opportunities. Our commitment is to be a reliable, forward-thinking partner for our clients, providing them with the means to thrive in an ever-evolving world.
