Suite IIZIPRO

Manage Your Business

This innovative solution integrates mobile and web applications to streamline your product offerings. The IIZIPRO suite provides a unified platform for both online and offline sales. It comprises the following applications:

  • IIZIMANAGER: Inventory and collection management
  • IIZICOM: Online store
  • IIZIMOBILE: Mobile management
  • IIZIRETAILS: Point of sale
  • IIZIBACKEND: Centralized management
IIZIMANAGER

Control Your Costs

IIZIMANAGER allows you to manage your inventory and collections precisely. Record future orders and optimize your pricing strategies.

Monitor daily, weekly, and monthly sales in real time, analyze sold items and cart value.

Keep an eye on your finances: expenses, cash flow, shipping fees, and taxes to ensure your business's profitability and transparency.

IIZICOM

E-commerce Site

Create a unique online presence with customizable website templates. EasyMakeMoney offers a variety of themes and color schemes that suit your brand.

Easily customize every component of your site with just one click to change its visual appearance at will.

These user-friendly and visually appealing templates let you perfectly reflect your visual identity and stand out in the digital market.

IIZIMOBILE

E-commerce Mobile App

Create a unique mobile experience with IIZIMOBILE, your e-commerce app for iOS and Android.

IIZIMOBILE offers several themes and color schemes to reflect your brand's visual identity.

Integrated with the same database as your e-commerce site, it ensures perfect synchronization of products, orders, and inventory in real time.

Provide your customers with a smooth and personalized mobile experience with a modern design.

Demo
IIZIRETAILS

Your Point of Sale

Manage your physical store with IIZIRETAILS, your connected point of sale solution.

IIZIRETAILS offers a customizable color palette to match your brand's visual identity.

Connected to the same database as your e-commerce site and mobile app, it enables synchronized management of sales, inventory, and orders.

Monitor real-time physical sales statistics and maintain precise control over major cash transactions such as closures, deposits, or withdrawals.

Demo
IIZIBACKEND

The Heart of Your Ecosystem

IIZIBACKEND centralizes all your sales platforms using a single database.

Connected to your e-commerce site, the IIZIMOBILE app, the IIZIRETAILS physical POS, and the IIZIMANAGER tool, it ensures perfect synchronization of products, inventory, orders, and transactions.

This robust solution guarantees real-time data consistency and simplifies overall management across all sales channels.
